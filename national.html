Medical City Hospital enhances Oman's health security, global standing :Health Minister

The Medical City Hospital for the military and security services enhances health security and places the Sultanate of Oman among advanced countries, said the Minister of Health. The opening of the Medical City Hospital for the military and security services comes to achieve a strategic importance that lies in the integration between all health institutions with all their military, security and civil branches and their cooperation in order to achieve their supreme goal embodied in health security, this is what His Excellency Dr. Hilal bin Ali Al Sabti, Minister of Health, stressed.

Oman participates in Sharjah Book Fair 2024

The Sultanate of Oman, represented by the Ministry of Information and the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Youth is currently taking part in the 43rd edition of Sharjah International Book Fair, the United Arab Emirates (UAE). The Sultanate of Oman’s pavilion at the fair includes diverse publications in literary, intellectual, artistic, scientific and historical disciplines. As many as 2,522 publishers and exhibitors from 108 countries are taking part in the fair, which runs till 17 November.
